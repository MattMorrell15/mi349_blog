About Adventure Interfaces

Adventure Interfaces is an in-depth look at some of the most important user interfaces used in human exploration. From the first navigational charts to modern GPS-these UIs have shaped human adventures for thousands of years. AI takes its research to the next level and looks closely at what makes each of these interfaces unique and usefu, how did it help accelerate human exploration?
